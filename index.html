About Us

E-Bazzar is Nepal's premier online fashion destination, bringing you the latest trends and timeless classics in clothing for men, women, and children.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various methods including eSewa, Fonepay, and Khalti.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Please contact our customer service for return requests.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within Kathmandu Valley takes 1-2 days, while outside valley deliveries take 3-5 days.
